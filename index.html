Crazy Cat Lady V2

  • High Concept:
  • Player Experience Goals: Experience a difficult action game feature projectile-physics based weaponry. Players will have to weave through enemy attacks while also using their movement to affect a trajectory to hit their target.
  • Genre: Action
  • Game play: Left and right arrows move character left and right, spacebar is jump, up is throw kitten. Wipe out the invading alien force to win.
  • Prototype goal: My original idea for the crazy cat lady game was to make something somewhat reminiscent of Super Ghouls and Ghosts (despite never having played it myself), however I ran our of time to consider enemy AI and I had stumbled upon something simplistic that was still fun. This time, though, I am returning to fulfill, to some extent, my original vision with enemies sporting multiple AIs. I only wish that I had left myself time to come up with a unique movement algorithm for the boss. Instead, it brings difficulty in its shooting pattern and ability to resurrect other aliens.
  • Features: Projectile kitten weaponry. Awesome chiptune music.
  • Setting/Premise: Aliens have invaded your town; you must save your town with nothing but an army of kittens at your disposal (because who doesn't have an emergency box of kittens in case of alien invasion?)
  • Target Audience: Fans of action games and those who always wanted to throw kittens at aliens.
  • Play time: Depending on the players skill, it could take several minutes. The last level has some difficult jumps that give players some trouble.
  • Strategies: Try to take out the aliens before they fire too many shots as dodging bullets can quickly get out of hand.
