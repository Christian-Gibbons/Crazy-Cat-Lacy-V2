Platformer V2

  • High Concept:
  • Player Experience Goals: A standard platforming affair that increases in difficulty as the game progresses
  • Genre: Platforming
  • Game play: Left and right arrows move character left and right, spacebar is jump. Hold down spacebar longer to jump higher.
  • Prototype goal: Polish up and improve upon my previous platformer.
  • Features: Directional collision, some difficult platforming.
  • Setting/Premise: You control a girl who is going around collecting gold coins. Funny thing about people who hoard pots of gold...
  • Target Audience: Fans of the platforming genre.
  • Play time: Depending on the players skill, it could take several minutes. The last level has some difficult jumps that give players some trouble.
  • Strategies: Platform well.
